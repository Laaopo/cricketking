Scoring App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Cricket King. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the integrity of your cricket scoring data. This Privacy Policy details how we handle user data. Our core philosophy is simple: your cricket data belongs to you.

100% Offline Data Storage

Cricket King is built as an offline-first application. To ensure reliable operations on local cricket pitches and remote leagues, all match scorecards, player statistics, and database entries are stored locally on your device:

  • No registration or accounts are required to use the scoring features.
  • Your match data is saved securely using a local SQLite database on your device's sandbox.
  • Automatic disaster recovery backups are saved locally as JSON files on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal identifying information (PII) or device identifier data. All information you input into the app stays strictly within the application boundaries on your physical device:

  • Match details: Scores, runs, overs, dismissals, and extras.
  • Player Profiles: Batting and bowling statistics, career milestones, names, and profiles.
  • Calculations: Team stats and Duckworth-Lewis-Stern (DLS) mathematical configurations.

Third-Party Services

Our application uses standard Android and iOS system components to operate, but does not embed third-party advertising SDKs or analytical trackers. The app runs free of external tracking components, giving you a smooth and clean experience.

Data Export & Sharing

When you use the PDF scorecard printing or CSV/Excel sharing features, the files are generated locally on your device's filesystem. They are only shared when you explicitly trigger the system share sheet (e.g., sending a scorecard to a league WhatsApp group or emailing the PDF score sheet).

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact details, we encourage you to review this page periodically within the app's website for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon publishing.
